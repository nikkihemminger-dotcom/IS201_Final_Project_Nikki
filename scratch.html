Favorite Running Routes

Three routes I've loved, ranked. Each has a few notes below it.

  1. Provo River Trail
    • Paved trail that follows the Provo River from Utah Lake up into Provo Canyon
    • Flat, scenic, and cool in the summer mornings
    • My go-to long run — I can stretch it to 15+ miles
  2. Y Mountain Trail
    • Steep switchback climb to the "Y" above BYU campus
    • About 2 miles round trip but it feels like more
    • Short workout with a real view at the top
  3. Carlsbad Beachwalk (Home)
    • Flat coastal path along the ocean in Carlsbad, California
    • Perfect easy recovery run over breaks
    • Sunsets from the seawall never get old
